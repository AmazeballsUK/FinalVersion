This package contains:
1) Game-ready low poly model of hazard sign.
2) Realistic looking substance what generate all textures for their material.
3) Painted concreate substance as bonus.

You can use one of 5 ready signs on your choice:
1) Flammable sign
2) Explosive sign
3) Corrosive liquid sign
4) Chemicalies sign
5) Exclamation sign
6) Or any your own image what was generate as hazard sign.

Features:
1) Rust spreading
2) Spreading smoothness
3) Vernish damage scale
4) Drips intensity
5) Drips samples amount
6) Drips smoothness

Hazard sign substance can by used with metallic and specular shaders.
